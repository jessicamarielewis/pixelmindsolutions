Refund Policy

At Pixel Mind Solutions, we strive to provide high-quality services that meet your expectations. However, if you are not fully satisfied with our services, we are here to assist you. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which you may request a refund for the services we provide.

1. Services Covered by this Refund Policy

This Refund Policy applies to the following services offered by Pixel Mind Solutions:

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Web Designing
  • Web Development
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Management
  • WordPress Solutions
  • Hosting Services

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

  • Service Cancellation: If you decide to cancel our services within 7 days of your purchase, you may be eligible for a full refund. After 7 days, refunds will not be issued unless otherwise agreed upon in a separate agreement.
  • Non-Delivery of Service: If we fail to deliver the services agreed upon in the service contract, you may be eligible for a refund. We will review the situation thoroughly to determine if a refund is warranted.
  • Technical Issues or Errors: In the event of a technical failure or error on our part that prevents you from receiving the services you paid for, we may offer a refund, depending on the circumstances.

3. Services Not Eligible for Refund

Certain services are non-refundable, including:

  • Completed Services: Once services such as website design, development, or SEO work are completed and delivered, no refunds will be issued.
  • Monthly Subscriptions: For services billed on a recurring basis (e.g., hosting services or ongoing SEO services), refunds are not available for payments already processed.
  • Custom Work: For custom web development, design, or SEO work where progress has been made on your project, refunds are not applicable after work has begun.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Reach out to us at pixelmindsolutions@gmail.com within 7 days of your purchase, clearly stating your reason for requesting a refund.
  2. Review: Our team will review your request and assess the situation. We may contact you for additional information if needed.
  3. Decision: Once your request is reviewed, we will notify you of our decision. If approved, we will process your refund within 7 business days.

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method, and processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

5. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer a partial refund based on the work completed up until the point of cancellation. If you have received any portion of the service, we will assess the value of the work completed and issue a refund accordingly.

6. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with the outcome of your refund request, we encourage you to contact us directly to resolve the issue. We are committed to ensuring that you have a positive experience with our services and will work with you to reach a fair resolution.

7. Changes to the Refund Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our refund procedures.
